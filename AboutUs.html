About Us

Polonez is Ireland’s leading Eastern European food chain, with **45 stores** across the country, including five in Northern Ireland, and we’re still growing.



For over **20 years**, we’ve been dedicated to providing a “Taste of Home” to Eastern European communities, while also serving as a gateway for Irish customers to explore the rich and diverse flavors of Eastern Europe.

Our Mission: We aim to **create a culinary bridge** between Eastern Europe and Ireland, offering a welcoming space where food becomes a celebration of culture, community, and comfort.


Whether you’re reconnecting with familiar traditions or discovering something new, **Polonez is here to bring people together through the language of food**.

Our Journey: We opened our first store in **2003 in Naas**. It was one of the first Polish stores in Ireland, and we aimed to deliver fresh, high-quality products from home while bringing a taste of Poland to Ireland.

Since then, we have expanded into **19 cities across Ireland and the UK**, including: Dublin, Cork, Limerick, Galway, Athlone, Bray, Carlow, Cavan, Gorey, Mullingar, Naas, Navan, New Ross, Portlaoise, Sligo, Tullamore, Lusk, Newry, Ballymena, Lurgan, Dungannon, Belfast.
